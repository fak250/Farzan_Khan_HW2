Theater as a Passion

I started doing theater as part of the production team as I had an audition fear. I started doing lights for my theater team and gradually became a permanent part of the team.

I started doing acting with a role in my school's annual play as the director held mandatory auditions so I had to overcome my fear to audition. This turned out to be a really important point as I went on to be a part of over 15 productions in the last 2 years of my high school.

The play most important to me was the one I wrote and performed with my friends protesting the extremism against Shias in Pakistan around 3 years ago.
